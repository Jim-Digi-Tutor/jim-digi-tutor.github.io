When you click your left mouse button once, it is known as a single left click, or just a left-click. Left-clicking does different things depending on what you are clicking on. For example, if you click a file, app, or folder, you will select it. Try it below.

Once you have selected an item you can do other things with it, such as rename the item, copy it, or delete it.
